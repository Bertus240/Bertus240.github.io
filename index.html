What is DALMA

Welcome to the world of DALMA, the first iconic celebrating Dalmatians. DALMA is a unique cryptocurrency rooted in community, heritage, and passion for this legendary dog breed. Launched on the KRC-20 blockchain, DALMA stands out with its community-driven approach, honoring the spirit of Dalmatians. Whether youre a crypto enthusiast or simply a Dalmatian lover, DALMA is made for you. DALMA is more than just a tokenits a symbol of loyalty, unity, and community, just like Dalmatians themselves. Every holder becomes part of our long-term vision. DALMA has a total supply of 28.7 billion tokens, designed for broad distribution while maintaining scarcity to support long-term value, DALMA is a 100% community-focused project, with the majority of tokens available through a fair minting system. This system allows anyone to join the DALMA ecosystem, with low barriers to entry, and ensures that early adopters dont monopolize the tokens!

Upcoming Plans

DALMA Minting

1 Kaspa (KAS) per mint.
Each mint provides 57,400 DALMA tokens.
A total of 475,000 mints ensures progressive and controlled distribution.

Premint Fund Distribution (5%)

Team: A portion will be allocated to developers and contributors for long-term motivation.
Liquidity (DEX): A reserve for liquidity to guarantee smooth trading on decentralized exchanges.
Marketing: Used for promotional campaigns to attract the crypto community and Dalmatian lovers.
CEX Listings: Funds reserved for securing listings on major centralized exchanges, increasing token visibility and liquidity.

Long-Term Vision

100% Community-Driven: DALMA is committed to being a project where the community holds power.
Strengthening Community Engagement: The project will host exclusive events, form partnerships with animal-related organizations, and run contests to maintain excitement and involvement.
Education and Adoption: We aim for DALMA to introduce newcomers to blockchain and cryptocurrency while staying accessible to experienced users.

DALMA Launch

Official launch on the KRC-20 blockchain.
